## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how MeJohnC.Org ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services at mejohnc.org (the "Service").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. Please read this policy carefully to understand how we handle your information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you create an account through our authentication provider (Clerk), we collect:

- **Email address** - Used for account identification and communication
- **Name** - Display name for your profile
- **Profile picture** - Optional, if provided through social login
- **Authentication tokens** - Secure session management

### Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our Service:

- **Page views and navigation** - Which pages you visit and how you navigate the site
- **Interaction data** - Clicks, scroll depth, and time spent on pages
- **Technical information** - Browser type, device type, operating system
- **IP address** - Used for rate limiting, security, and approximate location
- **Referrer information** - How you arrived at our site

### Content Data

If you are an authorized administrator, we store content you create:

- **Blog posts** - Titles, content, metadata, and publication dates
- **Projects** - Project descriptions, images, and related information
- **Site content** - About pages, work history, and portfolio information
- **Audit logs** - Records of administrative actions for security and accountability

### CRM Data (Administrators Only)

For business relationship management, administrators may store:

- **Contact information** - Names, emails, phone numbers, company details
- **Interaction history** - Meeting notes, communication logs
- **Business information** - Deal values, pipeline stages, follow-up tasks

### Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies and local storage for:

- **Analytics consent** - Remembering your consent preferences
- **Theme preferences** - Light/dark mode settings
- **Session management** - Keeping you logged in
- **Performance** - Caching preferences for faster page loads

## Cookie Policy

### What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites remember information about your visit.

### Cookies We Use

| Cookie/Storage | Purpose | Type | Duration |
|----------------|---------|------|----------|
| `__clerk_db_jwt` | Authentication session | Essential | Session |
| `analytics_consent` | Stores your analytics consent choice | Functional | 1 year |
| `theme` | Stores your theme preference (light/dark) | Functional | 1 year |
| `supabase_settings` | Development only: database configuration | Functional | Persistent |
| `_ga`, `_gid` | Google Analytics tracking | Analytics | 2 years |

### Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

1. **Browser settings** - Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies
2. **Consent banner** - Use our consent interface to manage analytics cookies
3. **Do Not Track** - Enable DNT in your browser to disable analytics

Note: Blocking essential cookies may affect the functionality of the Service.
